Mission

ClearRoots Foundation supports organizations that need reliable digital identity, secure access, and clear information delivery to reach learners, volunteers, and community partners. The focus is not only on technology, but on trust, continuity, and measurable field impact.

Infrastructure

This site is packaged as a custom Docker image, deployed to Kubernetes, published through Route53, and secured with automated HTTPS on AWS. The result is a low-cost but credible production-style architecture for a public-facing nonprofit platform.
